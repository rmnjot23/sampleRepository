^.^Welcome To Spot the Difference^.^

Instructions:

  1. Pick a Level: Easy, Medium or Hard.
  2. The first image is the one with out the changes.
  3. When you hover over a image in the level it will change to the one with change.
  4. You have to hover out of the image to start guessing the difference.
  5. Based on the time limit in each level set a timer and click out once the timer rings. :)

Easy

You have 5 seconds to Find the Difference
After that you have to take you cursor off the image.

Medium

You have 5 seconds to find the Differenc
After that move cursor off the image

Hard

You have 5 seconds to find the Differenc
